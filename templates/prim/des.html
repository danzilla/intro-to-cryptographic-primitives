Symetric Encryption

Symmetric encryption used to conceal the contents of blocks or streams of data of any size, including messages, files, encryption keys, and passwords.

DES Data Encryption Standard

DES is a symmetric encryption algorithm which essentially means it takes a single key to encrypt and the same key to decrypt. To use this encryption feature of our program it is important to keep the key a secret between you and the decryptor to ensure that the confidentiality of your message is kept. Take a look at the steps below to encrypt your message.
